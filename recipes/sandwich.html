Sandwich

Description

Easy, fast and healthy sandwich as a perfect snack

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of gouda cheese
  • 1 sea cucumber
  • cream cheese
  • salad

Steps

  1. put cream cheese on the bread slices
  2. put cheese slice on one of the bread slices
  3. put cucumber and salad on the cheese slice and then put the other bread slice on top of it
