Oat bowl

Description

Easy, fast and healthy oat bowl for a perfect breakfast

Ingredients

  • 125g oats
  • 500ml milk
  • 1-2 apples
  • 25g whey protein chocolate flavour

Steps

  1. cut apple into small pieces
  2. add oats, milk and apple pieces to the bowl
  3. put whey on top of it and mix it a little bit
