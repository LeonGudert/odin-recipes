Pasta Casserole

Description

Tasty, fast and easy pasta casserole

Ingredients

  • 400ml water
  • 100ml cream
  • 1 package of casserole flavour
  • 150g ham
  • 250g pasta
  • 100g cheese

Steps

  1. add water, cream and flavour to a pot, mix it together and let it cook for 1 minute
  2. put pasta in casserole, cut ham into small squares, add ham to casserole
  3. pour sauce from pot over the pasta and ham
  4. add cheese on top, let it bake for 40 minutes in the oven
